Lasagna Recipe

Description

Imagine this - you get home from a hard day's work and you're about ready for your afternoon cry. But you're hungry and you know your microwave broke yesterday. Pop this bad boy in your oven and you can cry in peace knowing you'll have some delicious lasagna at your fingertips.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 (16oz) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb shreeded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a largeskillet over medium-high het, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in saghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarell, Cheddar, and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13in pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture an cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bae in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
