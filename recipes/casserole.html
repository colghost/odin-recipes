Breakfast Casserole

Description

You've woken up from a hard night of table top Dungeons and Dragons and your stomach is growling for food. Instead of worrying about cooking some crazy meal, why not whip up a quick and easy breakfast casserole that's sure to excite and raise those hitpoints a few.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of homestyle biscuits
  • 12 pre-cooked sausage links cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 12 eggs, thoroughly beaten
  • 1 cup mild cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees
  2. Lightly grease a 9x13 baking dish
  3. Plop the biscuits in there and make it even
  4. Whisk the heavy cream into eggs and season with salt and pepper - then dump them over the biscuits.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until the eggs are cooked and biscuits don't feel doughy when poked.
  6. Allow the casserole to cool for ten minutes before serving
