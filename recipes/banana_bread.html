Banana Bread recipe

Description

Some days you want to eat bread, others you want to eat bananas, but what if you want to eat both at the same time? With this banana bread, you CAN! Bananas and bread don't really seem like they should go together, but who am I to judge? All I know is that its TASTY.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripre bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5in loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour; stir just to moisten.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65mins, until a tooktpick inserted into the center of the load comes out clean.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
